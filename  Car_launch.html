Features

Discover the innovative features of our latest car model.

Design and Build

The G-Class has a distinctive, boxy design that has remained largely unchanged since its inception, maintaining its iconic silhouette. It is built with a focus on durability and strength, featuring a robust body-on-frame construction.

Engine and Performance

The G-Class is powered by various engines, including a powerful V8 engine in the G550 model, which boasts a 3982 cc displacement and significant horsepower and torque for both on-road and off-road performance.

Luxury Features

Despite its rugged exterior, the G-Class offers a luxurious interior with high-end materials, advanced technology, and comfort features. This includes premium leather upholstery, state-of-the-art infotainment systems, and various customization options.

Off-Road Capability

Known for its off-road prowess, the G-Class comes equipped with features such as three locking differentials, a low-range gear, and exceptional ground clearance, making it capable of handling extreme terrains with ease.

Hybrid System

The latest models incorporate modern advancements like a 48-volt hybrid system that enhances performance and efficiency. For example, the 2025 G550 comes with a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine paired with this hybrid system, producing 443 hp and 413 lb-ft of torque.

Specifications

Get to know the technical specifications and performance details.

  • Engine: V8, 3982 cc, turbocharged
  • Horsepower: 416 hp @ 5,250 rpm
  • Torque: 450 lb-ft @ 2,000 rpm
  • Transmission: 9-speed automatic
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive
  • Fuel Economy: 13 mpg city / 17 mpg highway
  • 0-60 mph: 5.6 seconds
